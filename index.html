Save Energy Everywhere (S.E.E)

An automated lighting system module. To be implemented in all classrooms in high school.

  • Arduino-based solution

  • Relay implemented

  • Fully automated. Saves 5% of energy. Installed at JPIS Jaipur.

Speedbreaker Energy Generation (SEGx)

Prototyped a speedbreaker system which harnesses the energy lost on roads.

  • Converts the kinetic energy of a car's motion to tension and then to electrical energy using springs.

  • Can be used to power traffic-lights nearby. In talks with the JDA. (Jaipur Development Authority)
